About ISC

We are one of the leading manufacturers of LT Panels and PLC based Panels in Gujarat. Ours is a professionally managed company founded by a promising entrepreneur having four decades of manufacturing and field experience in India and abroad. Electrical Panel is the brain behind any system, whether it would be process, machines, or electrical distribution system. Precise design and engineering of panel is not a luxury but a basic requirement for the system. Designing a panel is an art and not just engineering.

When it comes to executing a challenging job with engineering excellence, ISC is one name that is always remembered not only by customers but also by competitors. Due to abundant experience in the whole gamut of applications, we have acquired a very distinct position in the otherwise crowded market of panel manufacturers. The quality of panels manufactured by us is one of the best in the industry. Quality is achieved not by accident but by meticulous execution of the job at every stage of engineering and manufacturing. We follow the well-accepted quality standards at every stage of execution.
